Support Our Cause

Your generous contribution helps us implement impactful projects and create positive change in our community.

The Rotaract Club of Freetown Sunset relies on donations from individuals, businesses, and organizations to fund our service projects and initiatives. Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a difference in our ability to serve those in need.

Your support enables us to provide educational scholarships, organize health camps, implement environmental conservation projects, and much more. Together, we can create lasting positive change in our community.

Donation FAQs

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, donations to the Rotaract Club of Freetown Sunset are tax-deductible in Sierra Leone. We will provide you with a receipt for your donation that you can use for tax purposes.

Can I specify which project my donation supports?

Yes, you can indicate your preference in the "Notes" section of the donation form or in the transaction note for bank transfers and mobile money donations.

How will I know my donation was received?

You will receive an email confirmation immediately after making an online donation. For bank transfers and mobile money donations, you will receive a confirmation email once the transaction is verified, usually within 2-3 business days.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Yes, you can set up monthly, quarterly, or annual recurring donations through our online donation form.

How is my donation used?

Your donation directly supports our service projects and initiatives in education, health, environment, and community development. We maintain transparent financial records and provide regular updates on how funds are utilized through our newsletter and annual reports.
