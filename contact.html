Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rotaract?

Rotaract is a service club for young adults aged 18-30 who are dedicated to finding innovative solutions to the world's most pressing challenges through service and leadership. Rotaract clubs are part of the global Rotary International family.

How often does the club meet?

Our club meets twice a month, on the 1st and 3rd Tuesday, from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM at the Radisson Blu Hotel in Aberdeen, Freetown.

How can I join the Rotaract Club of Freetown Sunset?

You can join by attending one of our meetings as a guest, then completing a membership application. Visit our Membership page for more details on the application process.

Are there membership fees?

Yes, there is an annual membership fee that helps cover club activities and administrative costs. The current fee structure will be discussed during your membership interview.

How can I volunteer without becoming a member?

We welcome volunteers for specific projects and events. Contact us through this form or email us at info@freetownsunsetrotaract.org to express your interest in volunteering.
