Our Story

The Rotaract Club of Freetown Sunset was established in 2010 with a vision to empower young professionals to become agents of positive change in their communities. Since our founding, we have grown into a vibrant community of dedicated individuals committed to service, leadership development, and international understanding.

As part of Rotary International's global network, we work closely with our sponsor Rotary Club and other Rotaract clubs to implement impactful projects and initiatives that address pressing social issues in Freetown and beyond.

Our Mission

To develop young adults as leaders in their communities and workplaces through service projects, networking opportunities, and professional development activities.

Our Vision

To be the leading youth organization in Freetown that empowers young professionals to create sustainable solutions to community challenges through innovation, collaboration, and service.

Our Values

Service Above Self, Integrity, Diversity, Leadership, and Fellowship guide all our actions and initiatives as we work to make a positive impact in our community.

Our Objectives

Community Service

Implement sustainable projects that address critical needs in our local communities.

Professional Development

Provide opportunities for members to enhance their skills and advance their careers.

International Understanding

Foster cross-cultural connections and global citizenship among our members.

Fellowship

Create a supportive network of like-minded individuals committed to making a difference.

Our History

2010

Foundation

The Rotaract Club of Freetown Sunset was chartered with 15 founding members.

2013

First Major Project

Launched our flagship "Education for All" initiative, providing scholarships to underprivileged children.

2015

Ebola Response

Mobilized resources to support communities affected by the Ebola outbreak in Sierra Leone.

2018

District Recognition

Received the "Outstanding Rotaract Club" award at the District Conference.

2020

10th Anniversary

Celebrated a decade of service with the launch of our environmental conservation program.

Present

Continuing the Legacy

Expanding our impact with innovative projects and growing membership.
